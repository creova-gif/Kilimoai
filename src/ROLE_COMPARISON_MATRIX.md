## рџЋЇ **Role Recommendations**

### **Choose "Smallholder Farmer" if:**
- 0-5 acres of land
- Managing farm alone or with family
- Need AI advisory + market access
- Want voice assistant (low literacy)
- Focus on single-family farming

### **Choose "Farmer (5+ acres)" if:**
- 5+ acres of land
- Independent farming operation
- Need data analytics (Farm Graph)
- Want financial management tools
- Scaling beyond subsistence

### **Choose "Farm Manager" if:**
- Managing multiple fields
- Coordinating a team
- Need task assignment tools
- Want advanced planning (mapping, allocation)
- Responsible for operational decisions

### **Choose "Commercial Farm Admin" if:**
- Running an enterprise farm
- Managing multiple farm units
- Need business intelligence
- Want agribusiness tools
- Require institutional reporting

### **Choose "Agribusiness Operations" if:**
- Buyer, supplier, or processor
- Operating in the marketplace
- Sourcing from multiple farmers
- Need procurement tools
- Focus on commerce, not farming

### **Choose "Extension Officer" if:**
- Working for NGO or government
- Advising multiple farmers
- Conducting field visits
- Assessing impact
- No direct farm ownership

### **Choose "Cooperative Leader" if:**
- Managing a farmer cooperative
- Coordinating group activities
- Handling member farmers
- Planning group sales
- Revenue distribution responsibilities
