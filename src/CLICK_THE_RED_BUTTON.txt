🔧 IF BUTTON DOESN'T WORK:

PLAN B: Hard Reload

  Chrome/Edge/Brave:
    1. Press F12
    2. Right-click reload button (↻)
    3. Select "Empty Cache and Hard Reload"
    
  Firefox:
    Press Ctrl+Shift+R (Cmd+Shift+R on Mac)
    
  Safari:
    Press Cmd+Shift+R
