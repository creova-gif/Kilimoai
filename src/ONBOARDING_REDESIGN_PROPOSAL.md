## 🚧 POTENTIAL RISKS & MITIGATION

### Risk 1: Less Data Collected Upfront
**Mitigation:** 
- Collect critical data later (progressive)
- Incentivize profile completion (+10 points)
- Gate advanced features until complete

### Risk 2: Users Skip Profile Completion
**Mitigation:**
- In-app prompts "Complete profile for AI recommendations"
- Gamification (progress bar, rewards)
- Email/SMS reminders

### Risk 3: OTP May Not Arrive
**Mitigation:**
- Show resend button immediately
- Add WhatsApp OTP alternative
- Allow email verification fallback

### Risk 4: Users Don't Understand Value
**Mitigation:**
- Personalized value prop (based on challenge)
- 5-second animated demo
- Social proof (10,247 farmers)
