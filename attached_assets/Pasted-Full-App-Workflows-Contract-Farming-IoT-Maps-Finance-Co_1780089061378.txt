Full App Workflows (Contract Farming, IoT, Maps, Finance, Community, and More)
Context

The app includes contract farming, IoT device integration (livestock monitoring, water levels, RFID gates), digital farm twin, satellite imagery, voice overs, marketplace (Pembejeo), task/notification page (Kazi), financial ledger (Daftari la Fedha), insurance, farmer groups (Vikundi), professional directory (Wataalamu), and a full crop library.
UI/UX must remain as is (light theme, Instrument Serif font, existing branding). We only add features and creative improvements on top – no dark mode, no font changes.

2. Data Management, Interpretation & Access Control
2.1 Data Collected by Devices
How is it interpreted?

Provide dashboards with raw values + AI‑generated insights (e.g., “Rumination down 20% – possible illness”).

Graphs, anomalies (e.g., sudden pH change in soil), and historical trends.

Exportability – allow export to CSV/JSON/PDF for farm owners.

Long‑term storage – data should not stay only on devices; sync to cloud with retention policies (e.g., 2 years for basic, unlimited for premium members).

2.2 Access Control (Commercial & Multi‑user)
Farm owner – full access.

Employees – role‑based access (e.g., vet sees animal health, mechanic sees equipment sensors).

Multiple devices per account – plans based on acres or headcount. Example:

Small farm (≤10 acres) → 3 registered devices.

Medium (11–50 acres) → 5 devices.

Large (50+ acres) → custom.

Allow the app to be installed on up to 3 additional devices per subscription tier (family/team plan).

2.3 Udongo & pH Page (Soil)
Show anomaly detection (e.g., “pH dropped from 6.8 to 5.2 in 2 days”).

Graphs over time (line, bar, scatter).

Sample suggestions – based on soil data, recommend fertilisers or crops.

Integration with IoT soil sensors (real‑time or periodic readings).
